Student Performance Prediction

Predicting academic success using supervised learning techniques.

Problem

Educational institutions often struggle to identify students at risk of failing. Without early detection, providing timely academic support becomes difficult.

Machine Learning Type

Supervised Learning — Regression

The model predicts a continuous value:
• Final Score (0 - 100)

Algorithms

  • Linear Regression: Simple and effective baseline model.
  • Decision Trees: Capture non-linear patterns.
  • Random Forest: Improves accuracy and robustness.

Input Data (Features)

  • Study hours per week
  • Attendance percentage
  • Assignments completed
  • Previous exam scores

Real-World Impact

Early Detection

Identify struggling students.

Better Support

Personalized academic help.

Performance

Improve student outcomes.
